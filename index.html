Frequently Asked Questions

How does the spam checker work?

Our spam checker analyzes your email content against thousands of known spam triggers and patterns. It checks for problematic words, phrases, and formatting issues that might trigger spam filters across different email providers.

Is there a free trial available?

Yes, we offer a free plan that includes 10 spam checks per day. For unlimited checks and advanced features, you can upgrade to our Professional or Enterprise plans.

How often is the spam database updated?

We update our spam word database weekly to ensure we're catching the latest patterns and triggers that email providers are using to filter spam.

Can I check emails for multiple providers?

Yes, you can select which email provider you want to optimize for before running the check. Each provider has slightly different filtering algorithms, and our tool accounts for these differences.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers for annual plans. All payments are processed securely through our encrypted payment system.
